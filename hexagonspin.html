Hexagon Spin

Hexagon Spin tests your reflexes and more. Your goal is to top your highscore and buy all of the shop items. The game is to slow and easy? Than try the new difficulties and if you are up for a challenge, than colormadness is for you. In easymode the puzzles come slow and verticly, so you have plenty of time to spin your hexagon to the right position. Mediummode is a much harder because the puzzles are not just faster, but they are also positioned horizontally. The puzzles get superfast in hardmode, but in the end of the game you get double coins and experience in hardmode than in easymode.

In the game you need to collect puzzles with your hexagon in the middle. Your hexagon has 6 sides, where on each side is a different shape and color. You turn your hexagon until the flying shape fits to the hexagon side. This takes some practise to get right, because is is only possible to turn your hexagon by moving you finger from LEFT to RIGHT or the other way around. After collecting the first coming blue rectangels you get a pointboost of 500 points and red triangels will appear more and more. That repeats until you unlock the last shape. After the last shape the the game speeds up rapidly.

Any Android phone can run this app, but sometimes the resolution is not adapted properly. The Plan for the next coming update is that Skins will be in the shop and a cat bundle will appear; the sounds will get better and more...
