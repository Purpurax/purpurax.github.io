Cube Timer

This is a Cube Timer for Cubers that want to improve their times on any Cube. Your times will be saved. You can track up to 13 cubes. If you are training for competition than this will help you with: inspection time, random scrambles, averages and more... If you want there also is a music funktion and a small alert that can reminds you after x seconds. Any computer can run this app, but it is recommended to use a resolution of 1920:1080 or higher. A window mode is included and a minimal mode will come soon. It is colorfull and has a dark mode too. I will work on improvements, just give me a feedback.
